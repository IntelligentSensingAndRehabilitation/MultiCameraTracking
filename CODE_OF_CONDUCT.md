# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing an open and welcoming community. We expect all participants to uphold this Code of Conduct to help us maintain a positive and inclusive environment.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- Focus discussions on the project and its goals
- Listen actively to others and value diverse perspectives
- Provide constructive feedback
- Acknowledge and learn from mistakes

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to identity or beliefs
- Unwelcome attention or advances
- Deliberate exclusion or intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spamming or trolling
- Any form of abuse or violence

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. Contacting the project maintainers directly
2. Email: peabody124@gmail.com, kshah@sralab.org

All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly. Retaliation for reporting in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are
not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban
temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem
inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

All community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the
reporter of any incident.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project-related communication channels
- Project events and meetings
- Any space representing the project or its community
